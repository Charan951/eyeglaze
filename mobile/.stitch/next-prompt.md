A premium eyewear e-commerce mobile home screen for "EyeGlaze" — a luxury eyewear shopping app.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Dark luxury e-commerce theme with ink-black background (#0D0D0D) and charcoal card surfaces (#1A1A1A)
- Antique gold (#C9A84C) as the primary accent for all CTAs, active states, and brand highlights
- Pure white (#FFFFFF) for primary text, smoke grey (#888888) for secondary/muted text
- Thin obsidian (#2A2A2A) borders on all cards and containers
- Generously rounded corners (12px) on buttons, cards, and inputs
- UPPERCASE section headings with letter-spacing, using extra-bold weight
- Full-width gold-filled primary buttons with black text
- Content-rich layouts with 16px screen padding
- No shadows — flat design with color-layered depth
- Premium eyewear e-commerce aesthetic — think luxury optical boutique
- Mobile-first design at 390px width
- Inter or system sans-serif typography

**Page Structure:**
1. **Top App Bar:** Left — circular profile avatar button with border. Center — "EYEGLAZE" logo in gold with "PREMIUM EYEWEAR" subtitle. Right — search icon and notification bell with gold badge showing "3".
2. **Hero Banner Carousel:** Full-width card (220px tall) with left-side text layout: gold subtitle "SEE THE WORLD", large white bold headline "CLEARER. SHARPER. YOU.", grey description "Premium Eyewear for Every Version of You", outlined gold "SHOP NOW →" button. Right side shows a model wearing glasses. Bottom-left has pagination dots (gold active, grey inactive). Auto-scrolling between slides.
3. **Category Grid — Eyeglasses:** Section title "EYEGLASSES". 4-column grid of square cards (3:4.2 ratio) for Men, Women, Kids, Rimless. Each card has a product image with gradient overlay and white label + gold "Shop Now →" at bottom.
4. **Category Grid — Sunglasses:** Same layout as Eyeglasses but for sunglasses categories: Men, Women, Aviator, Wayfarer.
5. **Featured Products Section:** "FEATURED" heading. Horizontal scroll of product cards showing product image, product name, SKU, price with strikethrough original price, and gold discount badge.
6. **Promo Banners:** Two promotional banners — "New Arrivals" and "Blue Light Collection" — with gradient backgrounds and CTA buttons.
7. **Bottom Navigation Bar:** 5 items — Home (active, gold), Wishlist (heart icon), center "GET GOLD" special button with gold gradient and star icon, Orders (bag icon), Wallet (wallet icon). Active items are gold, inactive are grey (#888888).
8. **Floating Cart Button:** Bottom-right floating action button with gold border, shopping bag icon, and gold badge showing cart item count.
